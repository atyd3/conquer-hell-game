    HOW TO PLAY?

    Choose from available difficulty levels or create a custom level.

    Select the monster that you want to fight with, each monster uses unique skills.

    Use mana points to make a special attack or heal. Use your HP to restore half of your mana points.

    You can use stun to stop some of a monster's special attacks while the monster is preparing to use them.

    Using the same skill as in the previous round will cost you a double mana points.

    A part of your mana points will be restored each round.
